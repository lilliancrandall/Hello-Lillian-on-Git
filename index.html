5 in 5

Each day, I receive a notification with a headline news story from the New York Times. For my 5 in 5 project I took a screenshot of the first alert I received each day and created an illustration through a Mandala technique using the Apple Pencil and iPad Pro.

    Constraint- Using the radial symmetry style of the Mandala Technique create an illustration
    Conditional- Using the New York Times headliner, create a mandala illustration
    to convey the headline.

Thursday

Day 1

On Thursday, the New York Times headlined an article about how counties that voted for Trump are doing in his economy which inspired my drawing to be an abstract expressionistic interpretation of the healdine. At first, I did this Mandala artwork with color, using lot of greens to symbolize money, in relation to economy. I felt this made the final series seem inconsistent.

Friday

Day 2

On Friday, the New York Times headliner an article about Paul Taylor who has deceased at 88. He was known for his beauitiful choreography and so I was inspired to draw a flowing, arbitrary mandala to convey the fluidity of dancing.

Saturday

Day 3

On Saturday, the New York Times headlined an article discussing Beto O'Rourke intentions to beat Ted Cruz in the race for Senate in Texas. Words that led me to draw a sugar skull/alien themed mandala included unapologetically, Democrat, win, trump country. These made me think of a dead waste land type of art to represent this headline. Again, with this mandala illustration, I chose to create an arbitrary interpretation rather a literal expression.
